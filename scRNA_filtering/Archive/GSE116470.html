Title A Single-Cell Atlas of Cell Types, States, and Other Transcriptional Patterns from Nine Regions of the Adult Mouse Brain
Organism Mus musculus
Experiment type Expression profiling by high throughput sequencing
Summary We report 690K single-cell transcriptomes from nine different brain regions from adult mice (Frontal and Posterior Cortex, Hippocampus, Thalamus, Cerebellum, Striatum, Globus Pallidus externus/Nucleus Basalis, Entopeduncular / Subthalamic Nuclei, & Substantia Nigra / Ventral Tegmental Area).
 
Overall design Single-cell suspensions were made from P60-70 C57BL/6 male mice (3-7 replicates / mouse) and sampled with Drop-seq. On average, each cell received 17.4 K Illumina sequencing reads from which we counted 2.2K transcripts and 1.2 K genes. In total, we measure 31.7K unique genes.
